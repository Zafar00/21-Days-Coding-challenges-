Paying attention on these bellow points can help in maintaining Healthy Habits

Eat Well

Include a variety of fruits, vegetables, whole grains, and lean proteins in your diet. Drink plenty of water and limit processed foods.

Stay Active

Engage in at least 30 minutes of moderate exercise most days of the week. It could be walking, jogging, or any activity you enjoy.

Get Enough Sleep

Ensure you get 7-9 hours of quality sleep each night. Sleep is crucial for overall health and well-being.

Manage Stress

Practice stress-reducing activities such as deep breathing, meditation, or spending time in nature.

Connect with Others

Build and maintain strong, positive relationships. Social connections contribute to a sense of belonging and happiness.
